A Note from the Creator

Why Juno?

"Hi, I'm Prasannajeet Ojha. During my studies, I constantly felt overwhelmed. I had dozens of research papers, lecture notes, and textbook chapters scattered across countless PDF files. Finding a specific piece of information meant opening file after file, using Ctrl+F, and wasting hours."

"I knew there had to be a better way. I imagined a tool where I could just 'talk' to my documents ask a question and get an answer synthesized from all my materials. That idea became Juno. I built it to solve your own problem, and I hope it can solve yours too."
